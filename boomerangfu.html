--Instructions--
Use Your Boomerang to Slice Your Opponent and Get Score.
Every Few Seconds A Power-Up Book Will Spawn They Can Carry Multiple Powers.
You Can Carry Up To 3 Powers at a Time, If You Collect
More Than 3 Power-Ups Then the First Power Up You
Collected Will Be Replaced by the New One
You Got.
--Controls--
==== P1 ==== (Sushi)
W - Move Up
A - Move Left
S - Move Down
D - Move Right
E - Throw Boomerang
Q - Dash
R - Recall Boomerang
==== P2 ==== (Watermelon)
Up Arrow - Move Up
Left Arrow - Move Left
Down Arrow - Move Down
Right Arrow - Move Right
"/" Key - Throw Boomerang
"." Key - Dash
"," Key - Recall Boomerang
==== Other ====
Space - Switch Stage
1 - Map Controls
2 - Spawn Power Up Book
--Power-Ups--
====Released====
Caffeinated - Move Twice as Fast
Disguise - While Not Moving or Holding Any Keys You Can Disguise into Your Environment
Teleport Boomerang - Teleport to Your Boomerang
Fire Boomerang - Leave A Trail of Fire Behind Your Boomerang That Wil Toast Enemies
