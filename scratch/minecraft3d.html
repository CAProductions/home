Controls
=============================
"W" • Forward
"S" • Backward
"A" • Move Left
"D" • Move Right
"⬆️" • Fly Up
"⬇️" • Fly Down
"M" • Toggle Mobile Controls
"F" • Toggle Info Bar
"V" • Toggle FOV And Variables
"1" • Switch to Empty Hand
"2" • Switch to Pickaxe
"3" • Switch to Axe
"4" • Switch to Shovel
"5" • Switch to Hoe
"6" • Unmapped
"7" • Unmapped
"8" • Unmapped
"9" • Unmapped
"T" • Open Command Center
"/" • Open Command Center
"enter" • Open Command Center

===============================

Adds A "Block" To the Specified Location
Example:
/setblock

Example:
